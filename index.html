Traditional Costumes

Wearing traditional clothes represents national identity and love for them. Many people wearing the same traditional clothes together gives a feel of patriotism as well as connectivity in culture and with each other as well as a sense of belongingness with the country.

Japanese Traditional Dress

The traditional dress of Japan is called kimono (和服).
South Korean Traditional Dress

The traditional dress of Korean is called hanbok (한복).
Burmese Traditional Dress

The traditional dress of Burmese is called longyi (လုံချည်).
Chinese Traditional Dress

The traditional dress of Chinese is called Hanfu (汉服)
